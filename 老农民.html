About Our Farm | 关于我们的农场

Gu Family Farm is a family agricultural business specializing in tomato and cucumber production. We manage the full agricultural process from seed selection and greenhouse cultivation to harvesting and market distribution.

谷氏家庭农场是一家家庭农业企业，专注于番茄和黄瓜种植。 我们管理完整的农业流程，包括种子选择、温室种植、收获以及市场销售与分销。
